Camera Settings

Camera Bounds restrict where the camera can move during gameplay. The camera will stay within the bounds you set, preventing players from seeing outside the play area.

Each map can have its own camera bounds. If no bounds are set, the camera freely follows the player.

Bounds Area

How to use:

  1. Click Set Bounds
  2. Drag on the map to select an area
  3. Drag again to expand the bounds
  4. Click Done when finished
